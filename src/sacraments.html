Sacraments

Baptism

Children: Please contact Sister Clemence Marie Mira, SPC, by calling the rectory office on Monday to Friday from 8:30 AM - 11:30 AM.

You can obtain an application form at the parish office on Monday to Thursday from 8:30 AM - 4:00 PM and on Fridays from 8:30 AM - 11:30 AM. The office is closed on Saturdays, Sundays, all Holy Days, and State and Federal holidays.

Interviews are available on the second Saturday of every month. At that time, you must bring all required documentation.

A Pre-Baptismal Catechesis for the parents and godparents is on the third Tuesday of every month at 5:00 PM in the Parish Hall.

Baptisms are held every on the fourth Sunday of the month at 10:00 AM after the 8:30 AM Mass. It is mandatory that the parents and godparents attend the 8:30 AM Mass before the Baptism. Baptisms are not performed during Lent.

Adults: For information regarding adult baptisms, please contact Ms. Fualupe Tuihalafatai, RCIA Coordinator, by calling the rectory office during office hours.

Confession/Reconciliation

The Sacrament of Reconciliation/Confession is offered on Saturdays from 5:00 PM - 5:30 PM or by appointment.

Please call the rectory office for more information.

First Holy Communion

If you would like to receive First Communion, please contact Sister Clemence Marie Mira, SPC, by calling the rectory office during office hours.

Candidates must be baptized and have received at least two years of catechism.

Confirmation

To learn more about Confirmation training, please contact Sister Clemence Marie Mira, SPC, by calling the rectory office during office hours.

Marriage

Couples interested in marriage are required to make arrangements at least four months prior to the wedding date.

Diocesan policy requires couples to attend an Engagement Encounter Weekend prior to marriage.

Please call the rectory office for more information.
